Welcome to DSC AE-FUNAI bootcamp!
We are excited that you have chosen to be part of this
exciting coding bootcamp experience. Expect to be
challenged. Our experienced mentors are ever ready
to guide you. Do well to read and follow the instructions
of this bootcamp. The bootcamp will last for 30 days.
Learners are expected to check in every day. Links to
the resources to learn from will be provided each day
and assignment(s) will be given according to what you
have learnt. You are expected to submit your assignment
and your mentors will mark and award scores.
